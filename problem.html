Accessibility Issues in Online Learning

The current state of online learning is riddled with accessibility issues. Millions with disabilities struggle with interfaces that don't adapt to their needs, hindering their education and career prospects. This is not just an inconvenience; it's a glaring inequality that demands a solution.

Common Barriers

  • Non-adaptive interfaces that are not user-friendly for individuals with disabilities.
  • Lack of multi-sensory learning tools, which makes it difficult for students with different learning styles to grasp the content.
  • Limited real-time support, which leaves many students without the help they need during critical learning moments.
